Most indie developers are good at building and terrible at distribution. That's not a character flaw. It's a skill gap — and it's exactly what we fix.

Nocturne runs a free 4-week program for developers with live apps. You bring your app and your creatives. We bring the publishing muscle — campaign setup, UA optimization, creative direction, and real budget for the best projects.

How it works

Open to any developer with a live app on the App Store or Play Store.
No pitch deck. No idea-stage projects. You need something live with a paywall before we can do anything useful together.

We are not a large publisher and that is intentional. You stay fully in control of your product, your content, and your communication. Nocturne handles the technical side — campaign setup, budget, measurement, optimization. Four weeks, then a clear answer.

W1
Acquisition

Nocturne injects an initial budget and launches the first campaigns. We measure what matters: CPI, install volume, early funnel behavior. Buying data, not users.

W2
Conversion — first read

We shift focus to the trial funnel. Trial start rate, trial-to-paid, early retention. We iterate on the paywall and creatives with what the data shows.

W3
Conversion — confirmation

We confirm the signal. Best creatives graduate to the trial campaign. We build a picture of what the cohort economics look like at scale.

W4
Decision

We review the numbers and give you a clear answer. If the signal is there, we make a formal proposal. If it is not, we tell you exactly why.

The deal

If we move forward after W4, the terms are always the same. No negotiation. No custom arrangements. No decisions based on who you are or how well you pitch.

  • 50/50 revenue share, lifetime. Nocturne runs all acquisition indefinitely. You keep building the product.
  • You own everything. Your app, your brand, your content, your communication. We handle the publishing layer only.
  • If we pass: you keep the data. Four weeks of real campaign numbers, yours to keep regardless of the outcome.

The deal is standard because we believe the value is in the execution, not the terms. A fair split applied consistently is better than clever contracts applied selectively.

What you bring

  • A live app with a paywall (iOS or Android)
  • Your own creatives — videos, screenshots, UGC. We give you direction, you produce the material.
  • Ownership of your content and communication. That side is yours, always.
