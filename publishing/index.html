Most indie developers are good at building and terrible at distribution. That's not a character flaw. It's a skill gap — and it's exactly what we fix.

Nocturne runs a free 4-week program for developers with live apps. You bring your app and your creatives. We bring the publishing muscle — campaign setup, UA optimization, creative direction, and real budget for the best projects.

How it works

Applications open to any developer with a live app on the App Store or Play Store.
No pitch decks. No idea-stage projects. Ship first, apply second.

W1
Go live

Your paywall is live and tracked. First creatives in production. We set up measurement together — installs, trial start rate, conversion. No guesswork.

W2
Test

A/B test your paywall and creatives. We give feedback on what hooks, what formats, what angles work for your category. You shoot, you iterate.

W3
Optimize

Double down on what works. Identify your best acquisition channel. Understand your CPI and what it means for the unit economics of your app.

W4
Present your numbers

One-pager: CPI, trial-to-paid rate, D7 retention. The projects with the best numbers move on. No judges. No vibes. Just data.

For the finalists

The top projects after W4 enter a 2-week publishing sprint run by Nocturne. No obligation to accept — but here's the deal if you do:

  • Nocturne puts up the acquisition budget — real money, real campaigns, on your app.
  • 2 weeks of live publishing — we run the campaigns, you watch the numbers.
  • If we continue: 50/50 revenue share, lifetime. Nocturne keeps running acquisition. You keep building the product.
  • If we stop: you keep everything. The learnings, the data, the installs. Zero strings.

What we expect from you

  • A live app with a paywall (iOS or Android)
  • Your own creatives — videos, screenshots, UGC. We help you make them better, we don't make them for you.
  • One weekly update posted to the community
  • Honest numbers. If the data isn't there, you're out. That's the deal.
